Detailed Comparison: Free Plan vs Paid Plan

Free Account Plan

Best suited for:

  • Beginner developers
  • Students learning about cloud
  • Startups building prototypes
  • Researchers needing temporary resources

Advantages:

  • No charges for 6 months
  • Full $200 credit
  • Automatic protection from charges
  • 30+ Always Free services
  • Auto-close when credit/time expires

Limitations:

  • No access to some expensive services
  • Cannot purchase Reserved Instances
  • Not eligible for other promotional credits
  • Automatic upgrade in certain cases

Paid Account Plan

Best suited for:

  • Production applications
  • Scaling beyond Free Tier
  • Enterprise customers
  • Need full access to AWS services

Advantages:

  • Full access to all AWS services
  • Full $200 credit
  • Short-term trials for select services
  • Eligible for other promotional credits
  • No auto-close of account

Disadvantages:

  • Charges apply when exceeding credit balance
  • Need careful cost management
  • Risk of unexpected charges
